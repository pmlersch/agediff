Variables Used in This Project

AGE person

Age of respondent. Integer years; top-coded at varying thresholds by sample. Missing = 999.

AGE2 (Age of spouse/partner) constructed

Age of the respondent's spouse or partner, linked via SPLOC. Used as age_sp in our data.

SEX person

Biological sex of respondent. Codes: 1 = Male, 2 = Female. Used to orient the age difference (husband − wife).

MARST person

Marital status. Key categories: married/spouse present, married/spouse absent, separated, divorced, widowed. We restrict to married with spouse present.

SPLOC constructed

Spouse's location (person number within the household). The pointer variable that links respondents to their partners, enabling construction of AGE2.

RELATE person

Relationship to household head. Used in constructing couple links and identifying spouse/partner pairs.

YEAR technical

Year of the census or survey. Our data spans 2005–2014. Combined with AGE to derive birth cohort.

COUNTRY technical

Country of census. Numeric codes based on ISO 3166. Our study uses 40 countries across all world regions.

PERWT technical

Person-level sampling weight. Indicates how many persons in the population each sample observation represents.
